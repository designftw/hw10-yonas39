Usability Issues

Introduction

Identifying and improving potential usability Issues is very important and impact the user's ability to use the chat app effectively. I have used Nielsen's heuristics as a guideline to evaluate the chat app's usability. I will present my findings and recommend solutions for the issues I have identified below.

Issue-1: Lack of help and documentation

  • Problem: Lack of help and documentation.
  • Heuristic:Help & documentation (L) - Nielsen
  • Description: The app needs to provide guidance or help for users who may be unfamiliar with the chat interface or features.
  • Severity: Major
  • Recommendation: Add a help section or tooltips with explanations for key features and functionality.

Issue-2: Error reporting, diagnosis, and recovery

  • Problem:Error reporting, diagnosis, and recovery are not present.
  • Heuristic:Error reporting, diagnosis, and recovery (S) - Nielsen
  • Description:The app does not provide any error handling or reporting, making it difficult for users to understand or diagnose issues when they occur.
  • Severity:Major
  • Recommendation:Implement error handling for common issues, such as network errors or failed message sending, and display appropriate error messages to users with guidance on how to recover or resolve the issue.

Issue-3: Visibility of system status

  • Problem:No notification for new messages.
  • Heuristic:Visibility of system status (S) - Nielsen
  • Description:The chat app does not provide notifications for new messages, making it difficult for users to know when they have received a new message without constantly checking the app.
  • Severity:Medium
  • Recommendation: Implement notifications for new messages, such as sound alerts, browser notifications, or visual indicators within the app.

Issue-4: Flexibility & efficiency

  • Problem:Lack of customization options.
  • Heuristic:Flexibility & efficiency (E) - Nielsen
  • Description:The chat app offers limited customization options, preventing users from personalizing the appearance or behavior of the app to suit their preferences.
  • Severity:Medium
  • Recommendation:Provide users with customization options, such as the ability to choose different themes, colors, or font styles, and to configure notification settings and other app behaviors.

Issue-5: User control & freedom

  • Problem:Users cannot undo or cancel actions.
  • Heuristic:User control & freedom (S)
  • Description:The app does not provide a straightforward way to undo or cancel actions like editing or sending messages.
  • Severity:Medium
  • Recommendation:Add a cancel button for editing messages and an option to undo sending messages within a short time window.

Issue-6: Error prevention

  • Problem:No error prevention for empty messages.
  • Heuristic:Error Prevention
  • Description:The app allows users to send empty messages without any content, which may lead to confusion or unintended message sending.
  • Severity:Medium /low
  • Recommendation:Add a validation check to prevent users from sending empty messages.
